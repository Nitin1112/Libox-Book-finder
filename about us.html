About:

Welcome to LIBOX, your community's hub for knowledge, exploration, and connection. Our library has been serving the Coimbatore community and we are committed to fostering a love for learning, supporting literacy, and providing access to a world of information and resources.

Our Mission:

At LIBOX, our mission is to "Enrich lives by promoting reading, lifelong learning, and cultural engagement". We strive to be a welcoming and inclusive space where everyone can discover, grow, and connect.

Our Values:

Accessibility: We believe in equal access to information and resources for all members of our community, regardless of age, background, or ability.
Community Engagement: We are dedicated to actively engaging with our community, listening to your needs, and evolving to meet them.
Education: We promote lifelong learning and offer a range of educational programs and services for all ages.
Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity and inclusivity, aiming to reflect the rich tapestry of our community in our collections and programs.
